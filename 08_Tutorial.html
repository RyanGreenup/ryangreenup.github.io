The /focus+context/ approach has the advantage that detailed information can be visualised in context of the entire visualisation, this however has the disadvantage that the view can be cluttered and appear noisy which may impede the focus of a user. *** Question 4 #+begin_quote Use TabuVis tool to open a tabular data set, performing filtering and zooming on the data set.
